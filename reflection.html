I applied to University of Cincinnati because I wanted to be in co-op program for Computer Science. I believe this would help me improving my resume by the time I graduate. So far, everything is enjoyable; I am on track with my career goals. I was lucky enough to land my first co-op in my second year with Fox Sports Cincinnati. Even though I was not be able to get to experience back end development at that time. However, through two semesters as quality assurance, I had learned more than I expected from testing website to how data flows behind it.

Not only my technical skills improved significantly through co-op, but also my communication and teamwork. Especially when my second semester was completely remote, communication was important to keep everything up to date and make sure everyone in the team knows what to do.

If I had a chance to start over again, I want to have a better preparation for my interview. Even though I landed the co-op, but I think my interview was not good enough.

My goal is keep gaining my experience about back end development through my next co-op and prepare myself better before graduation.
