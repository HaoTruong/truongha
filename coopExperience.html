Fox Sports Cincinnati

Quality Assurance

1/06/2020-05/01/2020

I was introduced into new Fox Sports website team as quality assurance intern. My work in this semester was focusing on testing tickets in each sprint. In the time when we did not have ticket to work on, I spent my time creating functions using C# to report our automation test runs to web-based test case management tool called TestRail.

I had a chance to learn more about website API, how to use them and what is behind the page of a website. As my goal is to become back end developer, learning how data flows behind the page helped me a lot to picture and reassure what I really want to do.

My communication also improved through out the semester. I had a hard time starting a conversation to asking for help when I was stuck with something. My supervisors helped me through the semester to communicate better. I believe it is really important to communicate effectively; It keeps the workflow going as fast as possible.
